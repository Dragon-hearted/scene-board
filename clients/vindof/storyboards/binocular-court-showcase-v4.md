## Style Anchor

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Warm neutrals — off-white, cream, warm grey
- **Secondary:** Muted earth tones — olive, slate, sand
- **Accent:** T-shirt colors punch through — red, blue, cream-yellow, grey graphic
- **Background/Neutral:** Concrete, asphalt, weathered bleacher wood — natural urban textures
- **Mood:** Warm, golden, slightly desaturated

### Photographic / Visual Style
- **Medium:** Photography — real, shot-on-location feel
- **Style Reference:** Lifestyle/documentary hybrid — like a friend filmed this, but with cinematic framing
- **Degree of Abstraction:** Photorealistic — grounded, authentic, no stylization

### Lighting
- **Overall Mood:** Warm golden hour — late afternoon sun, soft long shadows
- **Key Light Style:** Natural sunlight, soft and directional
- **Time of Day Feel:** Golden hour — warm tones, gentle lens flare welcome

### Camera Conventions
- **Primary Framing:** Mix — wide establishing, then medium/close-ups through binocular vignette
- **Movement Style:** Static to slow handheld — observer scenes steady, binocular POVs with subtle drift
- **Perspective:** Eye-level throughout — grounded, relatable
- **Binocular POV:** Circular vignette with soft dark edges, slight chromatic warmth, shallow depth of field on subject

### Character / Product Representation
- **People:** Young Indian men with fair skin (18-24), with distinct Gen Z styling — messy/textured hair (curtain bangs, grown-out cuts, bedhead waves), baggy bottoms (wide-leg pants, oversized shorts, jorts). Not polished, not athletic — the "I woke up like this but it's intentional" look. Natural, real faces — not model-perfect
- **Product:** The Vindof shirts ARE the product — clearly visible, well-lit, focal point in every binocular shot. Shown in natural context, never staged. The contrast between effortless Gen Z styling below the waist and the detailed Vindof pieces on top is the quiet statement
- **Brand Elements:** Vindof neck label visible in Scene 6 close-up. Vindof wordmark on final card. No forced logos elsewhere

### Overall Aesthetic
A golden-hour afternoon at an outdoor basketball court where the clothes are the most interesting thing happening. Young Indian guys just existing — messy hair, baggy jorts, but wearing pieces that reward a second look. The binocular device creates a curated, voyeuristic intimacy: you're noticing things that don't announce themselves. Quiet maximalism in motion — every detail earns its place, nothing screams.

### NanoBanana Preamble
"Golden hour outdoor basketball court. Warm natural light, soft shadows. Photorealistic documentary-lifestyle photography. Young Indian men with fair skin, messy textured hair and baggy bottoms, casual and authentic. Muted warm tones with garment colors as focal accents. Relaxed, effortless energy. No text in image."

## Production Notes

- **Music:** Chill, upbeat, casual track — should feel effortless, not high-energy. Think lo-fi meets soft hip-hop or indie electronic.
- **Color Grading:** Warm golden hour grade throughout. Slightly lifted blacks for a filmic feel. Desaturate slightly except for the Vindof garments — let the clothing colors punch through the otherwise muted palette.
- **Binocular Vignette:** Consistent circular frame with soft dark edges across Scenes 3-6. Slight chromatic warmth inside the circle. The vignette should feel optical and real, not a heavy-handed overlay.
- **Transitions:** Cut transitions throughout — no fades or wipes between binocular POV shots. The quick cuts build rhythm. Only the final wordmark uses a fade-in.
- **Pacing:** Deliberate open (3s) → binocular flashes accelerate slightly (2s → 1.5s → 1.5s → 2s) → quiet close (1s) → brand stamp (1s). Total ~13s.
- **T-Shirt Reference Images:** Product shots of all 5 Vindof pieces should be provided to the image generation pipeline as reference images to ensure garment accuracy.
- **Text Rendering:** All text (Vindof wordmark in Scene 8) handled via Remotion, not NanoBanana. NanoBanana prompts all end with "No text in image."
- **Video Generation:** All NanoBanana Pro stills (Scenes 1-7) are animated via Kling 3.0 image-to-video at 5s duration. Each scene uses a single consolidated Kling Prompt block containing the prompt text and a shared negative prompt. Scene 8 (wordmark) remains Remotion-only.
